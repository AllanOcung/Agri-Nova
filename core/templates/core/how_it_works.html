How It Works

For Farmers

  1. Step 1: Create Your Account & Farm Profile
    Sign up, set your location, select your crops, and add farm details to receive tailored advice.
  2. Step 2: Upload Crop Photos or Enter Field Data
    Upload crop images or enter field data (like temperature, rainfall, or soil moisture) for instant AI analysis.
  3. Step 3: Receive AI Predictions & Smart Advice
    Get feedback on crop diseases, pest threats, and receive real-time, localized advice.

For Agronomists

  1. Step 1: Build Your Advisory Profile
    Register, list your expertise, and connect with farmers based on crop types and location.
  2. Step 2: Review AI Reports & Farmer Submissions
    Access AI-generated predictions and farmer-submitted data to validate and support decisions.
  3. Step 3: Share Expert Advice
    Send personalized recommendations and enhance AI suggestions with your expertise.

For Extension Officers

  1. Step 1: Register & Define Your Coverage Area
    Sign up with your regional role and access the agricultural profile of your area.
  2. Step 2: Monitor Data Trends & Farmer Activity
    View real-time crop data, disease risk maps, and analyze engagement and health patterns.
  3. Step 3: Broadcast Alerts & Support Policy
    Send timely alerts to farmers and use AI reports for planning and support programs.
